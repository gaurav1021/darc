Some Disease Treated by Rheumatologist



Specializing in Treatment of all Rheumatic Diseases/ Autoimmune diseases/ arthritis/ musculoskeletal disorders.
The rheumatic diseases show peculiar presentations, multisystem involvement and chronicity. The need periodic monitoring and medications for long duration.
